Professional Interests

Robotics

I currently work for a robotics company, and my experience has given me a passion for learning about autonomous navigation. This is a really complex field which requires many layers of code to achieve this goal. Putting an autonomous robot in a retail space adds another large layer of complexity to the mix, as the robot has to be especially careful not to hit people. However, if the robot is too careful, it can get to the point where it just doesn't move at all because people are moving around it. This is a very common issue with companies developing autonomous retail robots.

The field of autonomous navigation is a very diverse and constantly changing one. This is why it is of particular interest to me. Self-driving cars, retail robots, warehouse robots, security robots, vacuum robots, aerial drones, self-driving boats, and many more sub-fields fall into this category. Each has their own set of unique issues, all of which I am fascinated by.

Medical Technology

As a follower of Christ, it's really important to me that what I do helps people who need it. There are so many incredible opportunities in the field of medicine for a person with computer hardware/software development experience and know-how. I would love to do work or research for developing products that will help save the lives of others.

In particular, I am very interested in continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) and electronic insulin pumps used to treat diabetes. These devices are quite amazing because they can save lives of people with diabetes and also improve their ability to keep their blood sugar at healthy levels which improves their A1C and overall health. As amazing as these devices are, there is also still a lot of room for improvement and I would love the opportunity to aid in this.

Restuarant Reviews

Restaurant Reviews

Some of my favorite places to go eat, in no particular order. Each restaurant gets a rating out of 5: 0 being the worst and 5 being the best. If you click on the image of the restuarant, it will take you to their website.
Restaurant Best Dish Overall Rating(Out of 5) Cost Review
Sicilian Pan Pizza 4.5/5 $10-$20 per person Joe B's is a great italian restaurant in downtown Lexington, KY. It features a tremendous variety of italian classics such as pizza, lasagna, cannelloni, fettuccine, manicotti, and a great deal more. The most notable item on the menu is the classic Joe B's breadstick. This breadstick is a staple of the restaurant, served with a delicious garlic butter. Joe B's is a wonderful spot to take friends and family for lunch or dinner.
Buffalo Chicken Taco 4/5 $10-$20 per person The Local Taco is fun restaurant in downtown Lexington, KY that features a wide array of tasty street tacos at a reasonable price. My go-to meal is a 4 taco basket with 2 steak tacos and 2 buffalo chicken tacos. The steak tacos have a tangy flavor coming from horse radish mixed underneath steak cooked with a chili spice rub. The buffalo chicken tacos come with a bit of a kick and a surprisingly satisfying mix of blue cheese sprinkled on top. This restaurant is a perfect spot for a date or a night out with friends as they also feature a full bar.
Red Beans and Rice 4.8/5 $8-$12 per person Bourbon 'n Toulouse is a quaint dive located near the University of Kentucky campus serving hot cajun food daily. Appearances can be deceiving and this restaurant is a prime example. It may not look like much from the outside, but when you step in and try their food, all preconceived notions will fade as their delicious cajin mixins' melt across your mouth. My personal favorite is the red beans and rice dish: a plate of red beans and savory sausage held together by a red sauce served on a plate of rice. The meal is further complemented with a couple of pieces of bread with a garlic sauce drizzled on top. I highly recommend this place for quick, delicious, and cheap comfort food.
