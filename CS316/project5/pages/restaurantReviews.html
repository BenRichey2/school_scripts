Restaurant Reviews

Some of my favorite places to go eat, in no particular order. Each restaurant gets a rating out of 5: 0 being the worst and 5 being the best. If you click on the image of the restuarant, it will take you to their website.

Restaurant | Best Dish | Rating | Price | Review

Buffalo Chicken Taco

4/5

$10-$20 per person

The Local Taco is fun restaurant in downtown Lexington, KY that features a wide array of tasty street tacos at a reasonable price. My go-to meal is a 4 taco basket with 2 steak tacos and 2 buffalo chicken tacos. The steak tacos have a tangy flavor coming from horse radish mixed underneath steak cooked with a chili spice rub. The buffalo chicken tacos come with a bit of a kick and a surprisingly satisfying mix of blue cheese sprinkled on top. This restaurant is a perfect spot for a date or a night out with friends as they also feature a full bar.

Sicilian Pan Pizza

4.5/5

$10-$20 per person

Joe B's is a great italian restaurant in downtown Lexington, KY. It features a tremendous variety of italian classics such as pizza, lasagna, cannelloni, fettuccine, manicotti, and a great deal more. The most notable item on the menu is the classic Joe B's breadstick. This breadstick is a staple of the restaurant, served with a delicious garlic butter. Joe B's is a wonderful spot to take friends and family for lunch or dinner.

Red Beans and Rice

4.8/5

$8-$12 per person

Bourbon 'n Toulouse is a quaint dive located near the University of Kentucky campus serving hot cajun food daily. Appearances can be deceiving and this restaurant is a prime example. It may not look like much from the outside, but when you step in and try their food, all preconceived notions will fade as their delicious cajin mixins' melt across your mouth. My personal favorite is the red beans and rice dish: a plate of red beans and savory sausage held together by a red sauce served on a plate of rice. The meal is further complemented with a couple of pieces of bread with a garlic sauce drizzled on top. I highly recommend this place for quick, delicious, and cheap comfort food.
